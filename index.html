Shichen Liu

Stealth mode. I was a senior research scientist at Google. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Southern California, VGL-ICT. Previously, I was in the Machine Learning Group, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Mingsheng Long and Dr. Yue Cao. I visited the Prof. Kilian Q. Weinberger’s lab at Cornell University, working with Prof. Gao Huang. My research explores principled methods for understanding the 3D world and creating advanced digital human technologies. I’m also a photographer, digital artist, and gamer.
